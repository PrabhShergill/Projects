ABOUT US


Legacy Martial Arts Consulting always aims at providing the best consultancy to martial arts business owners. Legacy Consulting always acts as a backbone for business owners and guides them towards the right direction.


Legacy Martial Arts Consulting was founded by Nick Castiglia, who has been training in Martial Arts since he was 10 years old. Nick runs a martial arts school and works as an instructor. He has over 20 years of experience in martial arts. The hardships faced by Nick has lead to the foundation of Legacy Martial Arts Consulting. He aims to provide businesses with exactly what they require for a clear path to success.
